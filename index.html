Why Jenneys College of Physical Education

Our mission is to provide quality education and to focus on holistic development of the student. Being a Physical Education College, our aim is to focus on Study - Theory and Practical - On the Field thereby moulding and shaping students to be qualified with excellence in becoming Sports Professionals, Educators, Physical Directors, Referees and Trainers. We also make major emphasis for personality development, communication skills and we provide extra classes for karate, sillambattam, taekwondo and physiotherapy etc.
