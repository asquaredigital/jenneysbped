B.P.Ed.,

Course Content

  • To conduct two seminars, training programme on Research Methodology for staff members.
  • To undertake at least five extension service activities and conduct a citizenship camp.
  • A Bridge course in English for the Physical Education Student Teachers.
  • To equip the psychology laboratory with more tools.
  • To conduct counselling programme especially psychiatric counselling to overcome problems and issues of the mind.
  • To conduct meeting for B.P.Ed. students through the Alumni Association.

B.P.Ed., Eligibility

B.P.Ed. Admissions are made strictly following the guidelines of the TNPESU. Both male and female candidates in the UG / PG degrees are eligible for admission to the B.P.Ed. two years degree programme.

Procedure for Admission

The completed application form for admission to B.P.Ed. courses shall be sent to the Principal. After proper screening and evaluation of marks, the candidates are provisionally selected for admission. The following original certificates with 3 sets of Xerox copies should be produced by the candidates at the time of admission. Incomplete applications will summarily be rejected.

Uniform

Students should compulsorily wear uniform during the working days of the college in order to maintain common discipline which may lead to effective character building and create equality among the students.
